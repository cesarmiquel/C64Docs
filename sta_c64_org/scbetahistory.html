History of The Star Commander beta releases

last updated on 2014-02-18

On this page, you can find the history of beta releases of The Star Commander and the Star Utilities.

Complete beta history

SC 0.90.01 beta (2011-02-06)

Changes since SC 0.83:

  • New: Introducing DOS 32-bit DPMI version.

  • Fix: No more lockups upon changing the drive on notebooks with a weird BIOS and no floppy drive.

  • Fix: Problems accessing the Windows clipboard have been fixed.

  • Fix: The viewer now correctly shows search results in files with extremely long lines.
