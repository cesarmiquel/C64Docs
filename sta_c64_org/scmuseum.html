The Star Commander museum

last updated on 2010-01-11

On this page, you can find old releases of The Star Commander and the Star Utilities.

Important notes

  • Do not redistribute! It is explicitly prohibited to publish these old releases on WWW sites, FTP sites, floppy disks, CD-ROM's or any other distribution media or to bundle them with hardware or software. Please, don't ask for permission, you will not get one. The only place you should get these old releases from is this page. If you find them somewhere else, contact the author.

  • Do not use for daily work. These releases are very old and may raise severe incompatibilities and other problems. If you find them do something better than a newer release does then contact the author, to help with making the newest release just as good.

  • Do not complain about problems. These releases are not maintained anymore so problems with them will not be solved.

  • Do not ask for the source. They're not available for the public.

In short, these releases are just for fun, nothing else. To see the complete list of changes since the very first release, see the history of changes. For the latest release, see The Star Commander homepage.
